## Printing Tips

**For Booklets:**

1. Print double-sided (flip on short edge)
2. Fold all pages in half together
3. Staple along the spine

**For Handbooks:**

- Print single-sided or double-sided as preferred
- Use standard letter paper (8.5" x 11")
