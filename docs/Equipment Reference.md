# Equipment Reference

Reference for weapons, armor, gear, and prices.

## Currency

- **Copper (cp)**: Common coin for everyday purchases
- **Silver (sp)**: 1 sp = 10 cp
- **Gold (gp)**: 1 gp = 10 sp = 100 cp

100 coins fit in 1 inventory slot.

## Usage Dice

Many items use **Usage Dice (UD)** to track quantity:

**d20 > d12 > d10 > d8 > d6 > d4 > depleted**

- Roll after use (ammunition: after combat)
- On 1-2, decrease die size
- When d4 shows 1-2, item is depleted
